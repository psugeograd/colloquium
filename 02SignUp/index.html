Science as Art Contest Entries¶

“Science as Art” is a new addition this year to the geosciences grad colloquium. The grad colloquium committee has defined the contest as a chance to show off your data visualization skills. The submissions should be a visual representation of a figure that you have made, whether it is a map, plot, or diagram. “Science as Art” will be different from the Photo Contest (which focuses on more geologic pictures, microscope images, field sites, etc).

“Science as Art” entries will be printed on a 20in x 20in piece of paper (in color). You may include a figure caption with the submission.

Submission Instructions

1. Title the Image File:

<lastName><FirstName>_Art.pdf

1. Submit The Photo to the following Email (optional figure caption in the email body):

geogradcolloquium@gmail.com. Format the subject title as "Art<lastName><FirstName>"
